# Knowledge Management in Action
## A Talk on Epistemology, Tacit Knowledge, and AI

Welcome to an exploration of how we actually create, share, and use knowledge—not through consultant frameworks, but through lived experience, conversation, and trust.

### What You'll Discover

1. **Foundations** - What is knowledge? How do we know what we know?
2. **Epistemology** - Different communities have different ways of knowing
3. **Core Concept** - Tacit knowledge: we know more than we can tell
4. **Critique** - Why popular KM models (SECI, DIKW) don't work
5. **Alternative Frameworks** - Mindfulness attitudes and adult development
6. **Practice** - What organizational knowledge really looks like
7. **New Paradigm** - AI as learning partner, not replacement
8. **Empowerment** - Why you should trust yourselves
9. **Meta** - How this talk itself demonstrates KM in action
